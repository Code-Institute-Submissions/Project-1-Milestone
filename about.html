Our little bookshop is a comfortable and queit place where you can browse our massive collection of books and sit down on one of our couches with a hot cup of coffee to read in peace. The smell of books mixing with the scent of cofffee and pastries, and the comfy cushions give our shop a unique and peaceful ambience.
Our little bookshop is located on the outskirts of Cork city center, near the river.
